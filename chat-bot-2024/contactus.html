Frequently Asked Questions

Quick Answers

Find immediate answers to common questions about our AI chatbot, features, and support options.

How does the AI chatbot work?

Our AI chatbot leverages advanced natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning algorithms to understand context, intent, and sentiment in your messages. It processes your input through multiple neural networks to generate intelligent, contextual responses in real-time, providing human-like conversations with instant access to knowledge.

Is my data secure and private?

Absolutely! We take data security and privacy seriously. All conversations are encrypted end-to-end, and we never store personal information without your explicit consent. Our systems comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other international privacy standards. Your data is never shared with third parties or used for training purposes without permission.

What are your support hours?

Our AI chatbot is available 24/7 for instant assistance. For human support, our expert team is available Monday through Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM EST. We typically respond to emails within 4 hours during business days and within 24 hours on weekends. Priority support is available for enterprise customers.

What languages are supported?

Our AI chatbot supports over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese (Mandarin), Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Russian, and many more. The AI can seamlessly switch between languages during conversations and maintains context across different languages for multilingual users.

Can I use the chatbot on my mobile device?

Yes! Our chatbot is fully responsive and optimized for all devices including smartphones, tablets, and desktop computers. You can access it through any modern web browser without needing to download an app. We also offer native mobile apps for iOS and Android with additional features like push notifications and offline message queuing.

What are the pricing options?

We offer flexible pricing plans to suit different needs: a free tier with basic features and limited conversations, a Pro plan for individuals and small teams, and Enterprise solutions with advanced features, unlimited usage, and dedicated support. All plans include our core AI capabilities with varying levels of customization and support.
